One integration. All of Web3.

Pheonixgives organizations the composable tools to launch blockchain products and services in a fast and seamless way. A developer-friendly SDK, wallet as a service, mobile and web connectivity, and security features make your product Web3-ready from day one. No custody of assets or on-prem implementation required.

PheonixSDK

The fastest, easiest integration for launching blockchain-enabled products.

  • Launch-ready in less than 100 lines of code and spin up proof of concept in less than 30 minutes

  • No dependencies or external libraries needed

  • React native, mobile-native (iOS, Android) and web-native SDKs

  • Supports ETH and EVM, ERC-20, ERC-721, ERC-1155 standard assets

PheonixWallet

Highly secure and UX friendly wallet as a service.

The PheonixWallet combines security and usability with a Threshold Signature Scheme (MPC) key management system and Account Abstraction powered smart contract functionality. A highly secure and user-friendly option for businesses to give users both full control of their assets and an excellent user experience.

Check Out Our Docs

Non-custodial wallets where user controls one of 2 signing shares through TSS MPC design

No seed phrases at onboarding which reduces the risk of users misplacing their keys!

Users will not lose access to their wallet with built-in recoverability. Shares can be backed up to iCloud or GDrive, or any storage system

Embedded, white-labeled wallet native to your app's UI/UX

Combine the scalability of TSS MPC key management and the usability of AA smart account functionality for the best UX 

Minimize vendor-lock in with a built-in eject private key feature

PheonixConnect

A wide range of blockchain products and services, out of the box.

PheonixConnect ensures your instance is fully functional. No additional integrations needed to access most ETH and EVM blockchains and dApps. Additional functionality is as easy as a simple call function.

  • Built-in EIP-1193 compliant web3 provider is configurable to any gateway provider and integrates natively with any protocol 

  • Curate access to hundreds of dApps within your own dApp store with a few lines of code

  • Use Wallet Connect to connect mobile wallet to desktop-based dApps

  • Build native protocol integrations on top of the web3 provider with one simple call function

PheonixSecurity FIREWALL

Security is in our DNA and at every layer our products.

  • Quarterly penetration tests and security audits conducted by external firm

  • Configurable firewall feature protects your users and helps you comply with regulations

  • Backup and recovery of shares automated through webhooks to enable simple set up and operation
